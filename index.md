# Hello!
I'm Carolina Cuadra Pardo, currently a Master's student at the University of Leeds, studying **High Performance Graphics and Games Engineering**

## Background
I came from Spain to the UK to study a **BSc in Computer Science at the University of Liverpool** , which I completed in 2023. I wanted to develop my interest for graphics and enrolled in the **University of Leeds**. 

## Skills
I enjoy most topics related to graphics, with an emphasis on geometry processing with meshes. Currently, I am working on my MSc Thesis on [**mesh reconstruction using marching cubes**](./MSC_THESIS.md).
